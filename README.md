\outl0\strokewidth0 \strokec2 # Ecommerce Customer Analysis & Spending Prediction\
\
## Project Overview\
This project analyzes customer behavior data from an e-commerce company to predict yearly spending. Using linear regression, we identify key drivers of customer expenditure and provide actionable insights for business strategy.\
\
## Key Insights\
- **Length of Membership** is the strongest predictor of yearly spending\
- **Mobile App** engagement has significant positive impact\
- **Website time** shows negligible correlation with spending\
- Model achieves **RMSE of $8.93** in predicting yearly expenditure\
\
## Business Recommendations\
1. Prioritize customer retention strategies to increase membership length\
2. Invest in mobile app optimization over website improvements\
3. Consider redesigning website experience to drive conversions\
